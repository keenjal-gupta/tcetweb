About Us

"Thakur College of Engineering & Technology (TCET), a Graded Autonomous Linguistic Minority Institute, was established in AY 2001-02 with a clear objective of providing Quality Technical Education in tune with international standards and contemporary global requirements."

Mission

"To provide state-of-the-art infrastructure and right academic ambience for developing professional skills as well as an environment for growth of leadership and managerial skills to students which will make them competent engineers to deliver quality results in industry."

Vision

"Thakur College of Engineering and Technology will excel in Technical Education to become an internationally renowned premier Institute of Engineering and Technology"
